The San Francisco Homebrewers Guild is proud to announce the 2016 CA State Homebrew Competition—the third and final leg of the CA Homebrewer of the Year Series.

Entry Registration Window 8/1/16–9/22/16 CS 212 Software Development
Entry Submission Window: 9/23/16 - 10/1/16 Entry Submission Locations: Ferment Drink Repeat (SF) and others TBD in near future Entry Fee: $10 Style Guidelines: 2015 BJCP Style Guidelines (We will be using "3. Styles Sorted Using Style Family" as defined in Appendix A of BJCP Style Guideline to establish our categories for competition) We will be holding two prelim sessions and 1 finals session this year Prelim 1: Saturday 10/15/16 @ Bare Bottle Brewing (exact times TBD) Prelim 2: Saturday 10/22/16 @ Ferment Drink Repeat (exact times TBD) Finals: Saturday 11/5/16 @ Anchor Brewery (exact times TBD)
